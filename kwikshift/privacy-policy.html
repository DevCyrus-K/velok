Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Kwikshift Movers website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, store, and protect information when you visit our website, contact our team, request a quote, or engage us for moving, packing, storage, or related services.

Kwikshift Movers is committed to handling your information responsibly and in a way that supports safe, practical, and reliable customer service. This policy applies to information collected through our website, enquiries, consultations, booking processes, and other direct interactions linked to our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We do not require users to create an account before viewing our website. However, we may collect information that you choose to submit through our forms, email, phone follow-up requests, or other direct contact points.

  • Contact form and enquiry submissions.
  • Moving quote, survey, or booking request forms.
  • Service request and consultation messages.
  • Feedback, survey, or support responses.
  • Newsletter sign-ups where available.
  • Job applications submitted through our careers page or related recruitment channels.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may use cookies and similar technologies to collect certain technical and usage information when you interact with our website.

  • IP address and device identifiers.
  • Browser type and version.
  • Operating system and platform details.
  • Referring website or traffic source information.
  • Pages viewed and time spent on pages.
  • Clickstream and general navigation patterns.
  • Date and time of visits.

3. Links to External Websites

Our website may contain links to external websites for convenience, reference, maps, tools, or related service information. These external websites may have their own privacy policies and operating practices.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit because we are not responsible for their content, privacy practices, security, or availability.

4. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect to support our services and improve your experience with Kwikshift Movers.

  • To respond to enquiries and provide customer support.
  • To prepare quotations and coordinate bookings.
  • To plan and deliver moving, packing, storage, and transport services.
  • To improve and personalize your experience on our website.
  • To analyze website usage and service trends.
  • To send updates, service information, or communications you requested.
  • To process transactions, applications, and service requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.
  • To improve existing services or develop new service features.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to unrelated third parties for their independent marketing use. We may share information only where needed to run our business, deliver services, or meet legal obligations.

  • With service providers: We may share relevant information with trusted providers, contractors, or operational partners who help us run our website, manage enquiries, or support service delivery, provided they handle the information appropriately.
  • For legal compliance: We may disclose information where required by law or in response to a valid legal request by courts, regulators, or public authorities.
  • Business transfers: If Kwikshift Movers undergoes a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that process.
  • To protect rights and safety: We may disclose information where reasonably necessary to protect our rights, investigate fraud, enforce our policies, or help protect the safety of our customers, team, or the public.

Where promotional messages are sent, they will only be based on your request, your consent, or another lawful basis that applies to the communication.

6. Data Retention

We keep the information we collect only for as long as it is reasonably necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, including handling enquiries, supporting bookings, maintaining records, improving operations, or meeting legal requirements.

  • The ongoing need for the information in relation to our services.
  • The nature of our relationship with you.
  • Any legal, tax, regulatory, or compliance obligations.
  • Legitimate business and operational needs.
  • Your consent where continued retention depends on it.

When information is no longer required, we may delete it, anonymize it, or securely limit access to it in line with our internal practices and legal obligations.

7. Safeguarding and Securing the Data

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to help protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information collected through our website and service processes.

  • Secure hosting, system controls, and appropriate access restrictions.
  • Reasonable protection of data submitted through our processes.
  • Administrative controls for staff or trusted partners handling information.
  • Routine maintenance, updates, and security checks where practical.
  • Backup and recovery measures for important operational data.
  • Response procedures for suspected misuse or security incidents.

Even with these safeguards, no transmission over the internet or electronic storage method is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website. If you believe a child may have provided information without appropriate consent, please contact us so we can review and remove the information where necessary.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our operations, services, technologies, legal requirements, or website practices. We encourage you to review this page whenever you visit our website.

Where material updates are made, we may post a prominent notice on the website or use another suitable communication method if we have your contact details. Continued use of our website after the changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to contact us about your information, please reach out by email at info@kwikshiftmovers.co.ke.

Questions About Your Privacy?

If you need clarification on how we collect or use your information, contact our team directly or review our cookie policy for more details about website data and consent.
